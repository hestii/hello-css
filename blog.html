Book a holiday to Bali for the same price as a trip to Spain


According to travel website TravelSupermarket, 2017 holiday bookings to luxury destinations like Crete, Santorini, Cape Verdi and Bali are experiencing a huge surge in interest. While traditional travel hotspots like Spain, Gran Canaria, Ibiza and the Costa Del Sol, getting less interest than in previous years.

According to experts from TravelSupermarket, money is the main reason for the shake-up. “Brits are clearly comparing what they can get for their money, realising that it doesn’t cost that much more to go further, as well as considering exchange rates”, a rep from the brand explains.
